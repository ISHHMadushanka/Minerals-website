EARTH PROCESSES

There are so many earth processes.Minerals and rocks also are caused due to earth processes.

Quartz

Quartz is a mineral composed of silicon and oxygen atoms in a continuous framework of silicon dioxide silicon-oxygen tetrahedra,with each oxygen being shared between to tetrahedra giving an overall chemical formula of silicon dioxide.

Apatite

Apatite is a group of phospate minerals , usually refering to hydroxylapatite , flouroapatite and chloroapatite, with high concentrations of OH-,F-,Cl- ions respectively in the cristal.

Hematite

Varieties include kidney ore martite , ion rose and specularite.While the forms of hematite vary , they all have a rust - red streak.Hematite is harder than pure ions,but much more brittle.
